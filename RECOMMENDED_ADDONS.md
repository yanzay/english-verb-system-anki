# Recommended Add-ons for the English Verb System Deck

This deck is **fully self-contained** and requires no add-ons to function. However, these add-ons enhance your study experience by complementing the deck's design, taxonomy, and production-oriented cards.

## Highly Recommended

### 1. Colorful Tags (+ Hierarchical Tags) — 594329229

The deck uses a deep hierarchical taxonomy with 13 modules and tag dimensions (`module:*`, `card-type:*`, `cefr:*`, `register:*`). This add-on makes the tag tree navigable and organized, letting you filter by specific modules, CEFR levels, or verb forms in seconds.

**Install:** Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons → paste **594329229** → OK

---

### 2. Review Heatmap — 1771074083

Maintains motivation during long-term retention study. The deck ships with an FSRS preset targeting 0.90 retention; this add-on visualizes your review consistency and streak progress across months of study.

**Install:** Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons → paste **1771074083** → OK

---

### 3. Speed Focus Mode — 1046608507

Perfect for production and reverse-production cards that use `{{type:Sample}}`. The add-on auto-reveals, auto-alerts, and auto-fails on timer, pushing you to respond quickly and strengthening active recall of verb conjugations and collocations.

**Install:** Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons → paste **1046608507** → OK

---

### 4. AnkiConnect — 2055492159

Essential for power users syncing with external dictionaries, clipboard tools, or corpus lookups during study. Ideal when studying collocations, phrasal verbs, and lexical patterns; lets you fetch definitions or usage examples without leaving Anki.

**Install:** Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons → paste **2055492159** → OK

---

### 5. Advanced Browser — 874215009

Leverages the deck's rich tag taxonomy to filter and sort cards powerfully. Search by `tag:cefr:b2 tag:module:listening` or any combination to study specific slices of the deck. Adds sortable columns for advanced card review workflows.

**Install:** Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons → paste **874215009** → OK

---

### 6. Pop-up Dictionary — 153625306

Provides quick inline lookups on words within cards. Useful for collocations and phrasal-verb cards where you want context-aware definitions without breaking your study flow.

**Install:** Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons → paste **153625306** → OK

---

## Not Recommended

The following add-ons may conflict with the deck's preset or harm learning outcomes:

- **SM-2 legacy helpers** — The deck uses FSRS, not SM-2. SM-2 add-ons override retention targets.
- **Ease resetters / reschedule agents** — These override the FSRS-optimized `desiredRetention: 0.90`.
- **Auto-bury siblings** — The deck's multi-cell cloze design relies on sibling relationships; burying breaks the design.
- **Anki-to-JSON exporters that auto-modify scheduling** — May lose FSRS history.

---

## Key Notes

- **No add-on required:** The deck ships with FSRS preset, `type:Sample` support, multi-cell cloze, and 13 curated modules covering basics through advanced discourse + per-L1 interference sub-decks.
- **Add-ons are optional:** They enhance workflow, filtering, and motivation, but aren't needed to study successfully.
- **Keep your presets:** Do not change "Desired Retention" from 0.90 or override the FSRS scheduler.

Happy studying!
